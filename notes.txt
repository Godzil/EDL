

Language notes

HANDLER.STATE.STATE...@

	Will only return true if we are in all the states

NEXT HANDLER.STATE.STATE...;

	Will set all states from top to last mentioned.

PUSH HANDLER.STATE.STATE...;

	Will push all states from top to last mentioned, also stores a depth marker into the HANDLER (required by POP)

POP HANDLER;

	WIll pop off states down to the depth marker in the HANDLER.
