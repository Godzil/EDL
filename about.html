Introduction

This is a work in progress account of the development of a language for writing emulators.

In the future I hope to have some actual documentation for EDL up on this website, along with a number of samples.

At present the language has allowed the development of a cycle accurate (down to the pins) Intel 8080 cpu. A Space Invaders emulator was then quickly written in C to prove the validity of the work so far.
